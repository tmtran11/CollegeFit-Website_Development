About

Using algorithms in Artificial Intellegience, CollegeFit is an model that will effective fit you with the National Universities in US based on the information that you provided. CollegeFit is different from a normal filter, as Artificial Intellegience provide it with the abilty to evaluate the hidden relationships between the information provided; therefore, is able to provide a much more reasonable, accurate and versatile fititng. 

The model used in CollegeFit has been trained on a database consists of 301 National Universities, with accuracy metric of 94%. We hope that CollegeFit will help you gain a general understanding of your standing and those of the National Universities, and finally, be able to give out helpful recommendations for your future education.
